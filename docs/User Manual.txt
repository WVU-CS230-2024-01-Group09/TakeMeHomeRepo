Welcome to the TakeMeHome app, a rideshare service designed for students at West Virginia University to offer and find rides home during breaks.


1. Account Creation and Login
   - To create an account, click on the "Sign up" link on the login page.
   - Enter your WVU email address (must include "@mix.wvu"), password, name, preferences (optional), phone number, and student ID.
   - After successful account creation, you will be redirected to the homepage.
   - To log in, enter your email and password on the login page.
   - If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password?" link and enter your email to receive a password reset link.


2. Homepage
   - After logging in, you will be directed to the homepage.
   - The homepage provides a brief description of the TakeMeHome app.
   - From the homepage, you can navigate to other sections of the app.


3. Adding a Listing
   - To add a new ride offer, click on the "Add Listing" button on the homepage.
   - Fill in the required information, including your name, trip date (must be a future date), destination, meeting time, meetup location, number of seats available, smoking preference, car type, and any additional notes.
   - Click the "Publish" button to submit your ride offer.
   - After successfully adding a listing, you will be redirected to the View Listings page.


4. Viewing Listings
   - To view available ride offers, click on the "View Listings" button on the homepage.
   - The View Listings page displays a list of all the ride offers.
   - Each listing shows the name, date, and location of the ride offer.
   - To view more details about a specific listing, click on the "View" button next to it.
   - In the detailed view, you can see additional information such as meeting time, number of seats, smoking preference, car type, notes, and the contact email of the listing creator.
   - To close the detailed view, click on the "X" button.


5. Account Page
   - To view and edit your account information, click on the "View Account" button on the homepage.
   - On the Account Page, you can see your email, name, preferences, phone number, and student ID.
   - To edit your account information, click on the "Edit" button.
   - Modify the desired fields and click on the "Save" button to update your account details.
   - To log out of your account, click on the "Log Out" button on the Account Page.


6. Error Handling
   - If you encounter any errors or issues while using the app, appropriate error messages will be displayed.
   - If an error occurs during account creation, login, or adding a listing, an alert message will be shown indicating the specific error.


Please note that the TakeMeHome app is designed for use by West Virginia University students only. Ensure that you use your valid WVU email address when creating an account.


If you have any further questions or need assistance, please refer to the app's documentation or contact the app's developers.


Enjoy using the TakeMeHome app and have a safe ride home!
